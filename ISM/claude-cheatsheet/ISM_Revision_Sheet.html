1. Descriptive Statistics

Mean
\(\bar{x} = \frac{1}{n}\sum x_i\)
Median
\[Middle value (sorted); average of two middles if n even\]
Mode
\[Most frequent value\]
Variance.*?Sample
\(s^2 = \frac{\sum(x_i-\bar{x})^2}{n-1} \quad \text{(sample — divide by } n-1\text{)}\)
Std Dev
\(\sigma \text{ or } s = \sqrt{\text{variance}}\)
Coef. of Variation
\(CV = \frac{s}{\bar{x}} \times 100\%\)

2. Probability Theory

Axioms
\(0 \le P(A) \le 1,\; P(S)=1\)
Addition Rule
\(P(A \cup B) = P(A)+P(B)-P(A \cap B)\)
Mutually Exclusive
\(P(A \cap B)=0\)
Independence
\(P(A \cap B)=P(A)P(B)\)
Conditional
\(P(A|B)=\frac{P(A \cap B)}{P(B)}\)
Total Probability
\(P(B)=\sum P(B|A_i)P(A_i)\)
Bayes
\(P(A_k|B)=\frac{P(B|A_k)P(A_k)}{\sum_i P(B|A_i)P(A_i)}\)
Naïve Bayes
\(P(Y|X_1,\ldots,X_n) \propto P(Y)\prod_i P(X_i|Y)\)

3. Random Variables

E[X] discrete
\(E[X]=\sum x\,p(x)\)
Variance
\(\mathrm{Var}(X)=E[X^2]-(E[X])^2\)
E[aX+b]
\[aE[X] + b\]
Var(aX+b)
\(a^2\mathrm{Var}(X)\)
Covariance
\(\mathrm{Cov}(X,Y)=E[XY]-E[X]E[Y]\)
Independence check
\(p(x,y)=p_X(x)p_Y(y) \;\forall x,y\)

4. Discrete Distributions

Bernoulli
\(P(X=x)=p^x(1-p)^{1-x},\; x\in\{0,1\};\; E=p,\; \mathrm{Var}=p(1-p)\)
Binomial
\(P(X=k)=\binom{n}{k}p^k(1-p)^{n-k};\; E=np,\; \mathrm{Var}=np(1-p)\)
Poisson
\(P(X=k)=\frac{e^{-\lambda}\lambda^k}{k!};\; E=\lambda,\; \mathrm{Var}=\lambda\)
Poisson approx
\(Use when n large, p small, np = λ\)

5. Continuous Distributions

Normal
\(X \sim N(\mu,\sigma^2),\; Z=\frac{X-\mu}{\sigma}\)
Z-table
\(P(Z>a)=1-P(Z<a)\)
t-distribution
\[Fatter tails; n < 30, \sigma unknown\]
Chi-square
\[Sum of squared Z — variance tests\]
F-distribution
\[Ratio of χ² — ANOVA\]

6. Smart Student Strategy

Keywords → Binomial
\["out of n", success/failure \to Binomial(n,p)\]
Keywords → Poisson
\["rate per hour" \to Poisson(\lambda)\]
Keywords → Normal
\["normally distributed" \to Z-score\]
Bayes hack
\[Tree diagram \to total prob denominator \to pick branch\]
At least one
\(P(X \ge 1)=1-P(X=0)\)
Sample variance
\[Always n-1 in denominator for sample data\]
